# 一只股票退市会怎么样？

> 原文：<https://lifehacker.com/what-happens-when-a-stock-is-delisted-1830279843>

每年，股票都会被各种交易所暂停交易和摘牌。虽然这种情况并不少见，但作为投资者，你肯定不想经历这种事情。因为最大的问题是:你的钱怎么了？

Watch

当 SEC 认为暂停交易符合公众的最佳利益时，它可以暂停交易。这包括了 [以下几种情况](https://www.sec.gov/oiea/investor-alerts-bulletins/ib_tradesuspensions.html) :

> *   Lack of up-to-date, accurate or sufficient information about the company, for example, when a company fails to submit periodic reports in time;
>     
> *   Questioning the accuracy of public information, including in company press releases and reports, about the company's current operating status, financial status or business transactions;
>     
> *   Issues related to stock trading, including insider trading, potential market manipulation, and clearing and settlement ability of stock trading.

虽然停牌解除后股票有可能再次交易，但有时股票会被摘牌，这对你的投资组合来说是个坏消息。

为什么？我们不是在讨论让一家公司私有化，这可能是一种很好的退市形式。在这种情况下，这可能表明你投资的公司有很大问题。虽然你不会马上“失去”你的钱——从技术上讲，你仍然是一个股东——但这些股票可能会变得一文不值。

“尽管自股票被摘牌的前一天以来，股票的内在价值没有变化，但它被逐出交易所的事实足以使市场因素将它的价格进一步推低，”华尔街 写道。“你曾经花血汗钱买的那只股票现在可能几乎一文不值。”

这并不是说你应该急于卖出，减少损失。如果该公司行动起来，股票有可能重新上市。如果公司申请破产，你可能会彻底倒霉。

如果你对一只股票感兴趣，但该公司过去曾被停牌，SEC 建议采取以下措施:

> *   *Research Company* : Be sure to research a company before buying its shares, especially after its suspension. Consider the company's financial, organizational and business prospects.
> *   *Review the company's SEC filing documents* : this information is free and can be found on the Commission's [Edgar filing system](http://www.sec.gov/edgar.shtml) .
> *   *Be skeptical:* Investors should always ask why someone gives them "hot" tips. Investors should also do their own research and realize that information from online blogs, social networking sites, or even the company's own website may be inaccurate and sometimes deliberately misleading:
